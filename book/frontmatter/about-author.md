# About the Author

**Terrence Battlehunt** is a network engineer and founder of Frontier Engineering, a software studio building AI-powered products for emerging markets. He splits his time between engineering infrastructure (Cloudflare, Docker, PostgreSQL, the things that keep systems running) and building AI workflows that turn domain expertise into automated systems.

Before writing this book, he spent years doing exactly what Chapter 1 describes — using AI through manual prompts, re-explaining context every session, and catching fabricated output by reading everything twice. The frustration of that workflow is what led to the systems approach this book teaches.

He trains Brazilian jiu jitsu, writes about engineering and AI on Twitter (@bmoreopensource) and Substack (Engineering Abroad), and is currently based abroad building products and raising his daughter.

This is his first book.
